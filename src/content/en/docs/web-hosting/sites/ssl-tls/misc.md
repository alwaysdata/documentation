## Cloudflare & SSL setup
 
When Cloudflare is set up in front of sites hosted on our servers, it handles the SSL.
 
Cloudflare has several SSL encryption modes, and you must choose the [Flexible mode](https://developers.cloudflare.com/ssl/origin-configuration/ssl-modes/flexible/) which is [compatible with our HTTP stack](/en/docs/web-hosting/sites/http-stack/).
