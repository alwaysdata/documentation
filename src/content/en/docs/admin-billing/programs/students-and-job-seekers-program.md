A **50% discount** off our [Public Cloud](/en/docs/admin-billing/billing/public-cloud-prices) plans is offered to students and job seekers. To take advantage of this discount, open a [ticket](https://admin.alwaysdata.com/support/add/) with proof for the current year:

- for students: certificate or proof of enrollment,
- for job seekers: proof of situation or receipt of allowance.

This proof will be required every year.
