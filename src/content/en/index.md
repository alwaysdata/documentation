## Start with alwaysdata

Learn how to use *alwaysdata*. Find out how to set up an account. Discover how to deploy a website/service in one minute.

- Find all of the [connection information](/en/docs/technical-specifications/login-details) linked to your account,
- Connect to your [administration interface](https://admin.alwaysdata.com),
- Create a [website](/en/docs/web-hosting/sites/add-a-site), [buy](/en/docs/domains/buy-a-domain) or [transfer](/en/docs/domains/transfer-a-domain) a domain, create [e-mail addresses](/en/docs/e-mails/create-an-e-mail-address),
- Transfer your files using a remote access protocol - [SFTP](/en/docs/web-hosting/remote-access/sftp) for instance - and create your [database](/en/docs/web-hosting/databases).

A problem? Contact our [support](https://admin.alwaysdata.com/support/add/).

Not yet a customer? Sign-up to our [platform](https://www.alwaysdata.com/en/register/) or contact our [sales department](https://www.alwaysdata.com/en/).

Want to show your support? [Give us some ❤](/en/love/) !

> [!NOTE]
Each time you encounter `[account]` you can replace it by the name of your account.

Any publication on Internet entails [legal obligations](/en/docs/web-hosting/sites/legal-requirements-on-internet), make yourself aware of them before placing your content on-line.
