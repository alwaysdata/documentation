Any website address hosted on alwaysdata servers **must** be declared in **Web > Sites**. Choose the [language](/web-hosting/languages) or type of you choice and configure them.

You can have as many sites as you wish in a single account. Note that this does entail a security risk as isolation takes place at the account level.
