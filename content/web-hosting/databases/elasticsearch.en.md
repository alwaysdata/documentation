## Connection

|||
|--- |--- |
|Server|localhost or 127.0.0.1|
|Ports|9200 & 9300 (Elasticsearch ports by default)|

Elasticsearch only runs in local mode with isolated permissions (separate user).

{{% notice info %}}
Elasticsearch is only available on [Private Cloud](/admin-billing/billing/private-cloud-prices) environments.
{{% /notice %}}
