This migration upgrades your databases to PostgreSQL version 18.

It introduces minor changes. To review them, go on the release note(s) involved by your current version (noticeable in the *Databases > PostgreSQL* section of your account).

- More information on the [version 18](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/18/release-18.html) and [release notes](https://www.postgresql.org/docs/release/)
