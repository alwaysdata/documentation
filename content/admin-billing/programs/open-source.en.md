A **[Small plan](/admin-billing/billing/public-cloud-prices)** is offered **free of charge** to active open source projects.

To take advantage of this plan, present us your project by contacting us by email (*community[at]alwaysdata.com*) or by opening a [support ticket](https://admin.alwaysdata.com/support/add/).
